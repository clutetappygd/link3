What is Interaction?

In a digital context, interaction refers to the way a user and a system communicate. This includes everything from the feel of a button press to the flow of an entire application. Good interaction design makes technology intuitive and enjoyable to use.

It's not just about aesthetics; it's about solving problems and guiding the user seamlessly towards their goals. A well-designed interaction can make a difficult task feel easy and natural.

The Importance of User Feedback

Effective interaction design relies on a continuous feedback loop. This means the system must respond to the user's actions, and the user must be able to provide feedback to the system.

Whether it's a loading spinner, a confirmation message, or a bug report form, feedback is crucial for building trust and improving the user experience over time.
